Welcome to our software development company! My name is Ernest Cronin, and I’m excited to share a bit about myself and the vision behind our work.

With nearly 20 years of experience in the software development field, I have dedicated my career to creating innovative solutions that meet the unique needs of our clients. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Philosophy and a Master’s degree in Computer Science, which gives me a unique perspective on problem-solving and technology. I am deeply passionate about understanding the underlying principles that govern both human thought and machine operation, allowing me to create software that is not only functional but also user-friendly and intuitive.

I have had the privilege of working in diverse industries, including healthcare and fintech, where I have honed my skills and gained valuable insights into the specific challenges and opportunities these sectors present. My commitment to excellence drives me to stay at the forefront of technological advancements, ensuring that our solutions are both innovative and effective.

Having lived in South Philadelphia since the 90s, I am deeply rooted in my community and strive to bring a local touch to our global operations. I believe that great software can emerge from a blend of local culture and the broader, fast-paced tech landscape.

Beyond my professional life, I am an avid traveler, and I aim to see the world—especially through my journeys in Latin America. Each trip expands my horizons and fuels my creativity, allowing me to bring fresh perspectives to my work.

In addition to my passion for software development, I am a jiu-jitsu black belt and have been actively competing for nearly two decades. I also train and corner several MMA fighters, which has taught me valuable lessons in discipline, resilience, and teamwork—principles that I carry into my professional endeavors.

I am also a dedicated chess player. In my earlier years, I earned a standard expert rating and enjoyed the strategy and challenge of the game. Although I have shifted my focus to blitz play, the critical thinking skills I developed through chess continue to influence my approach to software development.

At our company, we believe in a holistic approach to technology, where creativity, strategy, and user experience intersect. We are here to partner with you in navigating the complex landscape of software development and achieving your goals.
